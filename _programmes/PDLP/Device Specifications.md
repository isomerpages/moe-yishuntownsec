&nbsp;Should you have any questions or concerns, please contact our PDLP Hotline at&nbsp;&nbsp;**6853 8440**&nbsp;so that our ICT personnel can assist1&nbsp;you with checking your laptop requirements.

&nbsp;Please note that the PDLP Hotline operating hours is as follows:&nbsp;Weekdays 9.00 a.m. to 5.00 p.m. (Lunch hour: 12.00 p.m. to 1.00 p.m.)

**Note:**

**1\. You / your child may be required to bring the device to school to facilitate the checking of the laptop requirements by our ICT personnel. Do note that you / your child will need to be physically present during the check which will take about 15 minutes.**
