<p>Homecoming is a special occasion where we welcome back our alumni to YTSS.
It’s a time to reconnect, reminisce, and celebrate the enduring spirit
and bonds of our school community. As part of our YT40 celebrations, Homecoming
takes on even greater significance—bringing together generations of YTzens
to honour 40 years of shared memories, growth, and impact. Through meaningful
interactions, nostalgic moments, and renewed connections, our alumni are
invited to relive their YTSS journey while witnessing how the school continues
to evolve. More than just a reunion, YT40 Homecoming reflects our collective
pride and reaffirms the strong sense of belonging that continues to unite
past and present YTzens, as we look forward to the future together—YT40
&amp; Beyond.</p>
