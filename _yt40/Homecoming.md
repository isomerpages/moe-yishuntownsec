<p>Homecoming is a special occasion where we welcome back our alumni to YTSS.
It’s a time to reconnect, reminisce, and celebrate the enduring spirit
and bonds of our school community.</p>
<p>As part of our YT40 celebrations, Homecoming takes on even greater significance—bringing
together generations of YTzens to honour 40 years of shared memories, growth,
and impact.</p>
<p>Through meaningful interactions, nostalgic moments, and renewed connections,
our alumni are invited to relive their YTSS journey while witnessing how
the school continues to evolve. More than just a reunion,</p>
<p>YT40 Homecoming reflects our collective pride and reaffirms the strong
sense of belonging that continues to unite past and present YTzens, as
we look forward to the future together—YT40 &amp; Beyond.</p>
<p>Please join our telegram channel for YTSS alumni to send them updates
about YTSS, as well as connect with them and seek their support/help for
school events.</p>
