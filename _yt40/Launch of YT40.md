<p>Launched in 2025 by our former Principal, Mr Ng Teo Heng, YTSS’s 40th
anniversary—<strong>“YT40 &amp; Beyond”</strong>—marks not just a milestone,
but a forward-looking journey of reflection, pride, and renewed purpose.
More than celebrating our past, <strong>“YT40 &amp; Beyond”</strong> captures
our commitment to continue growing, evolving, and shaping the future together.
It honours our collective resilience and the strong foundations built over
four decades—shaped by generations of YTzens, where every small action
contributes to a greater whole.</p>
<p>This launch sets the stage for a series of celebrations in 2026, where
the spirit of <strong>“YT40 &amp; Beyond”</strong> will come alive across
the school community. As we commemorate 40 years of impact, we also look
ahead with confidence—continuing to inspire one another and building a
stronger, more connected, and brighter future for all YTzens.</p>
