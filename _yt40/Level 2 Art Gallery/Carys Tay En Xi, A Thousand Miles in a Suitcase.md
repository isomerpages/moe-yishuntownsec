<h4>Carys Tay En Xi, <strong><em>A Thousand Miles in a Suitcase</em></strong>, oil on canvas</h4>
<p>This painting explores the notion of displacement within an unequal social
landscape.</p>
<p>The privileged live freely, surrounded by travel, comfort, and choice,
untouched by hardship while the refugees carry their homes in a single
piece of luggage, holding survival, identity, and loss. The artist reflects
personally, where her own “luggage” contains childhood memories and innocence.</p>
<p>Through this juxtaposition, the work invites viewers to consider the privileges
often taken for granted, and to reflect on what “home” truly means when
it is no longer a place, but a condition shaped by circumstance, memory,
and resilience.</p>
