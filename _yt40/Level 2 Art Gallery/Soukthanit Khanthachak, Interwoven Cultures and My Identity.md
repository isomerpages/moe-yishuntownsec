<h4>Soukthanit Khanthachak, <strong><em>Interwoven Cultures and My Identity</em></strong>, Mixed media on canvas</h4>
<p>Growing up surrounded by diverse cultures—Thai, Singaporean, Chinese,
Vietnamese, and Laotian—I have developed a deep appreciation for their
unique identities. Through this artwork, I explore the harmony created
when these influences come together, celebrating the richness, beauty,
and interconnectedness of multicultural experiences that shape both my
perspective and artistic expression.</p>
<p><strong><u>See and Wonder:</u></strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Which part of the artwork reminds you of your own culture or experiences?</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Why do you think the artist combined animals, flowers, and people?</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>What story do you think is happening between the different elements in
the artwork?</p>
<p></p>
</li>
</ul>
