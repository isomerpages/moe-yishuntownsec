<p>This exhibition presents students’ artworks exploring contemporary issues
such as identity, culture, digital influence, and aspirations. Through
personal narratives and visual expression, it invites reflection on growth
and challenges. The showcase encourages students to consider how they can
mature, adapt, and transform meaningfully as they navigate an ever-evolving
future with resilience and purpose.</p>
