<h4>Fatimah Zahrah Bte Abdul Aziz, <strong><em>Between Two Worlds</em></strong>, oil on wood</h4>
<p>This artwork contrasts the calm simplicity of kampung childhood with the
intensity of modern city living. Divided by a central highway, it reflects
a journey between past and present. Caught between nostalgia and progress,
the piece expresses uncertainty while embracing adaptation within an ever-changing,
fast-paced world.</p>
<p><strong><u>See and Wonder:</u></strong>
</p>
<p>1) How does your parents' or grandparents' childhood differ from yours?</p>
<p>2) Would you prefer living in the modern times or in the past?</p>
<p>3) Do you have any fond memories from the past that you wished to revisit?
Why?</p>
<p><strong><u>Activity:</u></strong>
</p>
<p>Compare your environment from the past to the present. Draw and write
a short description of your fondest memory from the past.</p>
