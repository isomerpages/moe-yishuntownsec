<h4>Teo Soon Hang,<strong><em> I Define My Success</em></strong>, watercolour on paper</h4>
<p>This artwork reflects a youth’s perception of success shaped by wealth,
travel, and a glamorous career. Surrounded by global icons and currency,
his exaggerated laughter blurs the line between genuine joy and a performative
mask—revealing how survival in the corporate world often demands the illusion
of happiness.</p>
